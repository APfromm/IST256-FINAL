SERVICE AT PFROMM ELECTRONICS


Pfromm Electronics prides itself on providing exceptional service and repair work for all home electronics, including all radios and all televisions. Our team of skilled technicians is able to handle a wide range of issues, from simple tune-ups to complex repairs. With a deep understanding of both vintage and modern equipment, we ensure that every device, whether it’s a classic tube radio or a modern flat-screen TV, receives meticulous attention and the finest care. Customers can expect comprehensive diagnostics, transparent communication, and reliable solutions that stand the test of time. Pfromm Electronics uses only the highest quality parts for replacements, and all repairs come with a satisfaction guarantee. We also offer in-home services for larger items, making the repair process as convenient as possible. Whether it’s restoring a cherished family heirloom radio to its former glory or fixing a cutting-edge television, Pfromm Electronics is dedicated to keeping your home entertainment systems in tip-top working order.
