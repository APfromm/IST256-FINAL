SUPPORT FROM PFROMM ELECTRONICS


At Pfromm Electronics, we stand behind the quality of our products and services. Our repair and return policy is designed to ensure your satisfaction and confidence in our offerings.

Repair Services:
In-Store & In-Home Repairs: We offer both in-store and in-home repair services for all electronics. Our technicians are trained to handle a variety of issues, ensuring your products are functioning as intended.
Warranty Coverage: Products under manufacturer or seller warranty will be repaired at no cost. We will handle all warranty communications and services on your behalf.
Out-Of-Warranty Repairs: For items out of warranty, we provide a detailed quote before any work is done. All repairs are guaranteed for 12 months.

Return Policy:
30-Day Return Window: You may return products within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, exchange, or store credit, provided they are in the same condition as sold, with any included packaging or accessories.
Restocking Fee: A maximum restocking fee of 10% may apply for returns made after the 30-day period or if items are not in similarly good condition as sold.
Defective Products: If you receive a defective product, please report it to us within 5 days of receipt for prompt resolution.
Refunds: Refunds will be processed in the original form of payment. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.
Exchanges: For defective or incorrect items, we offer immediate exchanges to ensure minimal disruption of your entertainment experience.

Customer Support:
Contact Us: For any repair or return inquiries, please contact our customer support team. We are here to assist you every step of the way. We value your business and are committed to providing you with the best possible service. Thank you for choosing Pfromm Electronics.
