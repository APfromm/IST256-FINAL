ABOUT PFROMM ELECTRONICS


Pfromm Electronics has been offering the best in Over-The-Air entertainment solutions to Berks and beyond since 1926. With a rich history that spans nearly a century, Pfromm Electronics is a testament to the enduring value of quality and service. As a family-owned business, we have provided generations of families with the greatest in home electronics. From the radios that connected households to the world, to the televisions that brought cinematic experiences into the living room, Pfromm Electronics maintains an unmatched commitment to excellence. Our knowledgeable staff, many of whom have been with us for decades, are passionate about helping customers find the perfect product to fit their lifestyle. Whether it’s a state-of-the-art television, or a vintage-style radio for those who appreciate the nostalgia of yesteryear, Pfromm Electronics caters to all. In addition to our extensive product range, Pfromm Electronics offers personalized installation services, ensuring that every customer enjoys a seamless setup experience. Our post-sale support and repair services are unmatched, reflecting our dedication to customer satisfaction long after a purchase is made.
